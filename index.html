Keys to my ear

Musical key describes the scale on which a song is based. This means that most of the notes in a song will come from the scale of that key. In addition to my spotify wrapped 2020 -en 2021 playlists, the dutch top 100 of the top40 playlist and the Billboard top 100: 2021 are used for the key analysis

The barplot on the left shows the percentage of songs in each key that come from each playlist(key distribution across playlists). According to the spotify API, the integers map to pitches using standard Pitch Class notation. E.g. 0 = C, 1 = C♯/D♭, 2 = D, and so on. If no key was detected, the value is -1. It appears that I only have one song with the key-integer value of 3, in tonal, D-sharp/E-flat. The song in question is “Right Above It” by Lil Wayne and Drake

“Right Above It” is Lil Wayne’s first single from his fresh-out-of-prison album, I’m Not A Human Being. It’s produced by the legendary Kane Beatz of “We Be Steady Mobbin” fame. As of 2015, the song is used as the opening theme to the HBO series Ballers. -Genius Annotation

Chroma -and Timbre Self-Similarity Matrices “Dark Horse” -Katy Perry, Juicy J

One song I liked to listen to in the gym in 2021 was ‘Dark Horse’(2013) by Katy Perry and Juicy J.

Commercially, the song became Perry’s overall ninth number one single on the Billboard Hot 100, and her second from PRISM, as “Roar” reached the top spot months prior.

due to the “Trap”-type beat and the verse of Juicy J. The chroma-based matrix of the song, picks up mostly the chorus of the song. In the timbre matrix, one can see the beginning of every verse and the end of the chorus(tiny square that gets faintly darker) clearly.

Chroma -and Timbre Self-Similarity Matrices “Power” -Kanye West

Another song I like to listen to in the gym is ‘POWER’ by Kanye West.

During an interview with New York radio station Power 105.1, West said he spent thousands of hours working on this carefully-crafted song. “A song like ‘Power’ took 5,000 hours, like literally 5,000 man-hours to do this one record. That’s the amount of time I was putting into every song on the album,” West revealed. The accompanying music video has received critical acclaim. Russian supermodel Irina Shayk, who sits in a chair in the video, has called it “a moving painting”. -Genius Annotation

This masterpiece, contains a lot of hidden messages in the lyrics and it contains vocals in the background almost throughout the whole song, this can be seen by the big square in the chroma matrix.

Another part of the song that is picked up by the chroma matrix, is the part after the bridge. Here, right around 3:20 the track seems to slow down for a moment, and then it continues with the normal speed. In the timbre matrix one can see the beginning and end of each verse.
